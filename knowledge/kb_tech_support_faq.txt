# Account and Technical Support Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find quick answers to common technical and account-related issues.

---

## **Q: How do I change my password?**

**A:** You can change your password by logging into your account, navigating to the **"User Profile"** page, and selecting **"Security Settings."**  
You will need to enter your old password for verification.  
If you have forgotten your password, use the **"Forgot Password"** link on the login screen.

---

## **Q: My game won't launch. What should I do?**

**A:** This is often caused by driver issues or missing dependencies. Try the following steps:

- **Restart Your PC:** This solves most temporary issues.  
- **Verify Game Files:** In your Library, select the game and run the **"Verify Installation"** tool to check for corrupted or missing files.  
- **Update Drivers:** Ensure your graphics card drivers (NVIDIA, AMD, Intel) and operating system are fully up-to-date.  
- **Check Minimum Specs:** Make sure your computer meets the game's minimum system requirements.  

---

## **Q: Can I share my account with a friend?**

**A:** No. GameVerse Terms of Service prohibit sharing account login details.  
Your GameVerse account and game purchases are for **your personal use only**.  
Sharing credentials can lead to account suspension.

---

## **Q: How can I view my purchase history?**

**A:** Your full purchase history — including dates, prices, and transaction IDs — is available on the **"User Profile"** page under the **"Order History"** or **"Transactions"** tab.
