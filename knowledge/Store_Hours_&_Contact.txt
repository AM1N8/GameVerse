# GameVerse Store Information and Contact Details

This document provides details on when you can access GameVerse and how to reach our support team.

---

## **Store Hours**

GameVerse is open **24 hours a day, 7 days a week (24/7)**.

As a digital webstore, our game catalog, purchasing, and game library access are always available.

---

## **Customer Support Hours**

While the store is always open, our human customer support staff operate during the following hours:

- **Monday – Friday:** 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (Local Time)  
- **Saturday – Sunday:** Closed

For general policy questions, please use the **GameVerse AI assistant** first, as it can provide instant answers 24/7.

---
