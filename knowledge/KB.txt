# GameVerse Refund and Return Policy

At GameVerse, we strive for customer satisfaction with every digital purchase. Please read our primary refund policy carefully.

---

## **Standard Digital Game Refund**

We offer refunds for most digital games purchased on the GameVerse platform, provided they meet **all** of the following criteria:

- **Time Limit:** The request for a refund must be submitted within **fourteen (14) days** of the purchase date.  
- **Playtime Limit:** The game must have **less than two (2) hours** of playtime logged on your account.

If a game meets both of these criteria, you are eligible for a full refund back to your original payment method.

---

## **Exceptions (Non-Refundable Items)**

The following items are generally **not eligible** for a refund:

- Games exceeding 2 hours of playtime  
- Games purchased more than 14 days ago  
- Gifts that have been redeemed by the recipient  
- In-game consumables, currency, or items (unless required by law)  
- Third-party purchases made outside of the GameVerse platform  

---

## **How to Submit a Refund Request**

To initiate a refund, please follow these steps:

1. Log into your GameVerse account.  
2. Navigate to the **"Purchase History"** section.  
3. Select the game you wish to refund and click the **"Request Refund"** button.  

If your purchase is not eligible, please contact our human support team if you believe a special circumstance applies.
